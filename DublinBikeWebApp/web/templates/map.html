Up-To-Date Map Information

Below you will find all the information you need regarding bike stations and their location, as well as the ability to get directions between stations!

Please take a look at the key for guidance on how to read our Bagel Bike map. They are colour blind friendly!

Please also take into account that bikes are only avilable between 5am and 12:30am daily, however they can be returned at anytime 24/7.

Our dark blue bagel indicates that a bike station is completely empty (meaning there are no available bikes)

Our light blue bagel indicates there are a few bikes available and many open spaces

Our pink bagel indicates that the station is mostly full with bikes and there are few open spaces

Our dark red bagel indicates that the station is full with bikes and there are no open spaces
